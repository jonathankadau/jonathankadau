WHO AM I

I am an architectural designer currently studying at SCI-Arc. My work explores the relationship between form, systems, and infrastructure, often through speculative and diagram-driven projects.

I am interested in architecture as a tool that operates beyond buildings, engaging logistics, territory, and political systems. My projects frequently examine how architectural form can both organize and reveal hidden processes.

This portfolio is a curated selection of academic work, research, and experimentation across drawing, modeling, and representation.
