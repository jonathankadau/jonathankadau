KYOGRE X-500

Type Studio / Speculative

Focus Oceanic megastructure

Keywords flotation, machine, territory

KYOGRE X-500 is a speculative floating machine operating within the Pacific Ocean. The project imagines an infrastructural entity that occupies, extracts, and stabilizes territory while remaining untethered to land.

The project draws from Archigram-era megastructures and contemporary logistics platforms, treating the ocean as a spatial and political field rather than a void.

This moment in the project marks a shift from overall massing to internal systems. The drawings below focus on section logic, environmental exchange, and structural continuity across the form.
